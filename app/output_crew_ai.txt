The following laws govern property matters in India: 
1. The Transfer of Property Act, 1882 
2. The Special Relief Act, 1963 
3. The Indian Contract Act, 1872 
4. The Registration Act, 1908 
5. The Indian Stamp Act, 1899 
6. The Indian Succession Act, 1925 
7. The Indian Easements Act, 1882 
8. The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 
9. Urban Land (Ceiling And Regulation) Act (ULCRA), 1976 (Repealed w.e.f. 22nd March, 1999) 
10. Land Acquisition Act, 1894 
11. The Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (w.e.f. 1st January 2014) 
12. State/Local Laws
